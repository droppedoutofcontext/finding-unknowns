What is this?

A live example from the finding-unknowns skill. People are bad at describing visual taste and very good at recognizing it, so instead of asking "what style do you want?" the skill renders the same screen four genuinely different ways and lets you point. The chips let you compose a fifth direction out of pieces; the textarea at the bottom writes your next chat message for you.
